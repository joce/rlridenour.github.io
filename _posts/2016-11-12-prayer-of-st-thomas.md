>Grant me, O Lord my God,  
>a mind to know you,  
>a heart to seek you,  
>conduct pleasing to you,  
>faithful perseverance in waiting for you,  
>and a hope of finally embracing you.

<cite>St. Thomas Aquinas</cite>
